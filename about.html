Who We Are

Where Kerala Tradition Meets Arabian Hospitality

Al-Wazaan Restaurant & Coffee Shop was founded on a simple belief — that good food should feel like home, wherever you are. Nestled near Muscat Bank in Firq, Nizwa, our kitchen blends the comforting flavours of Kerala cooking with the rich, generous spirit of Arabic cuisine.

From steaming idiyappam and banana-leaf meals to charcoal grills, biriyani, shawarma and our famous fresh juices, every dish is prepared the way we'd serve our own family — fresh, flavourful, and full of care.
